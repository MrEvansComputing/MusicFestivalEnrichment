What you need to do

  1. Go to Canva and create a poster design.
  2. Add your festival logo.
  3. Add the festival name, date, time, location and ticket information.
  4. Add headline acts and 3 reasons people should attend.
  5. Use music-themed images, colours and fonts that match your brand.
