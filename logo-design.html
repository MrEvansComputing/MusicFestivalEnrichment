What you need to do

  1. Go to Canva and create a new logo design.
  2. Use your festival name or initials.
  3. Choose colours and fonts that match your festival style.
  4. Include a simple music or festival-related visual idea.
  5. Keep the logo clear, readable and not too crowded.
